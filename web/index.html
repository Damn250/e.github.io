Problem Statement

Children in South Sudan are not going to school due to an unimproved education system as it does not offer quality education for students. This will create a gap in the labor force as most of the children take on farming as it is their family's ancestral tradition. Most of the labor force is concentrated in a few certain fields especially farming which leaves deficiencies in other fields and depreciates a country's economy.
